Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light" and is famous for its art, architecture, culture, and cuisine. It offers a perfect blend of historical landmarks, romantic ambiance, and vibrant street life.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic iron structure that defines the Paris skyline.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A Gothic architectural wonder with stunning stained glass windows.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A historic avenue known for shopping and scenic strolls.
  5. Montmartre – A charming hilltop district known for art, cafes, and the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A flaky, buttery pastry that's perfect for breakfast.
  • Ratatouille – A flavorful vegetable stew originating from the Provence region.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate cookies with creamy fillings.
  • Coq au vin – A hearty French dish of chicken braised in red wine.
  • Crème brûlée – A rich custard dessert with a crisp, caramelized sugar top.
